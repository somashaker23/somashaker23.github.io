Logolinism
Anatomy of the mark, rules for its use.
The connected-node mark is a precise geometric construction. These rules ensure it stays consistent everywhere it appears.
Structure
Five nodes form two converging branches meeting at a single decision point (E). The mark is perfectly symmetric around its vertical centre axis at x=60.
Node Position Radius
A — top-left (16, 18) 7
B — mid-left (30, 52) 6
C — top-right (104, 18) 7
D — mid-right (90, 52) 6
E — decision (60, 96) 9
Edge Angle Length
A → B 67.6° 36.8
B → E 55.7° 53.3
C → D 112.4° (mirrored) 36.8
D → E 124.3° (mirrored) 53.3

Using the VOCAB-AI logo
Do
Use the provided SVG files — never recreate the mark.
Maintain the correct aspect ratio at all times.
Use the full-color version wherever possible.
Place on solid, uncluttered backgrounds.
Respect the minimum clearspace (X) on every side.
Pair the mark with the VOCAB-AI wordmark in Sora 700.
Don't
Recolor the mark or change the orange accent.
Stretch, skew, rotate, or distort the mark.
Add drop shadows, gradients, outlines, or effects.
Place on visually busy backgrounds or photography without a solid container.
Animate or alter individual nodes.
Pair with any typeface other than Sora for the wordmark.

Attribution rules
•  Always use the complete "Powered by [mark] VOCAB-AI" lockup — never just the mark alone.
•  "Powered by" is set in Inter 500, 11px, uppercase, muted colour.
•  Minimum mark size in attribution context is 16px.
•  Do not place inside a container smaller than 180px wide.
•  The attribution badge must have its own clearspace — treat the entire badge as a single unit with X/2 padding around it.
